Registry – The Blockchain Central

How the Registry Works

The Blockchain Central provides a neutral, verifiable registry for public and permissioned blockchains to be recognized and referenced across jurisdictions.

1️⃣

Submit a Blockchain

Institutional actors or open-source teams submit a blockchain system for peer review.

2️⃣

Verify & Register

Independent validators check authenticity, vote, and sign consensus for trusted inclusion.

3️⃣

Publish to the Registry

Verified blockchains are added to the global open registry — public, immutable, and auditable.
