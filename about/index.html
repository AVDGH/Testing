Who’s Behind The Blockchain Central?

TBC isn’t a crypto startup. It’s not a DAO. It’s not another blockchain. It’s the missing infrastructure — a public, verifiable registry where institutions, developers, and citizens can discover which blockchains are trusted, by whom, and why.

TBC is a neutral organization building trust infrastructure for blockchain systems. Our mission is to bridge decentralization with institutional trust — without compromising either.

👥 Who We Are

We’re a diverse team of technologists, policy thinkers, open-source developers, and public sector allies. We don’t aim to own the ecosystem — we aim to anchor it.

Our backgrounds span government, banking, cybersecurity, digital identity, and blockchain R&D. What unites us is a shared mission: building public trust infrastructure for a decentralized world.

🌍 What We Believe

Decentralization needs trust anchors. Transparency beats assumption. Institutions and open systems must learn to work together.

TBC is committed to neutrality — we don’t rank blockchains, issue tokens, or chase hype. We verify, register, and maintain a provable record of trust.

💡 What We’re Building
  • A registry blockchain for validating chains
  • Tools for institutions and governments to reference trusted chains
  • Open APIs, proofs, and decentralized governance standards
  • A global validator community anchored in transparency

We’re just getting started — and we’re building it in the open.
