📚 The TBC Registry

A public, cryptographically verifiable registry of trusted blockchains. The Blockchain Central (TBC) Registry is the core of what we do...

🧱 What’s in the Registry?

Each entry in the TBC Registry includes:
✅ Blockchain Identity
🏛 Institutional Signatures
🧾 Governance & Compliance Metadata
🔐 Cryptographic Proofs
🌍 Jurisdictional Tags

🔍 Why It Matters

Right now, trust in blockchain relies on audits, reputation, or just hope. TBC brings proof and provenance...

🛠 How It Works

1. Submit — Institution provides docs and chain specs
2. Validate — Validators cryptographically approve
3. Register — Chain is published and queryable via API/UI/CLI

🔗 Built for Interoperability

The registry doesn't replace chains — it makes them visible:
• APIs
• CLI tools
• Public viewer
• Cryptographic proofs

📣 Want to List a Chain?

If you represent a blockchain or institution, submit to TBC.
All validation is public — no gatekeeping, no rankings.
Just cryptographic clarity.
